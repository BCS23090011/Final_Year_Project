Project Overview

This project develops a machine learning framework to understand why Genshin Impact players churn and how to predict at-risk players — combining survey analytics, unsupervised clustering, classification models, and SHAP explainability.

01

What

A complete player analytics pipeline — from survey design to ML models — covering active player retention and churned player behaviour using n=170 survey respondents.

02

Why

Traditional analytics fail to capture the dynamic, multi-dimensional nature of player engagement. Churn causes in free-to-play games remain poorly understood despite massive economic impact.

03

How

CRISP-DM framework combining K-Means clustering, Self-Organising Maps, Logistic Regression, Random Forest, and SHAP explainability — with 4-platform community sentiment analysis.
